Target Variable

X5GPAALL: Postsecondary Transcript: GPA at all known institutions attended

Predictor Variables

  • X4ENTRYMAJ23: Major considering upon postsecondary entry- 23 categories
  • X1LOCALE: School locale (urbanicity)
  • X1CONTROL: identifies the sample member’s base year school as being a Public, Catholic, or Other Private School, as indicated in the source data for sampling: the Common Core of Data (CCD) 2005-2006 and the Private School Survey (PSS) 2005-2006.
  • X2ENRSTATSCH: School provided student enrollment status
  • X1SCHOOLCLI: This variable is a scale of the administrator’s assessment of his/her school’s climate. Higher values represent more positive assessments of the school’s climate (i.e. fewer problems are indicated).
  • X1SCHOOLBEL: Scale of student’s sense of school belonging
  • X3TGPAACAD: GPA for all academic courses
  • X1RACE: Student’s race/ethnicity-composite
